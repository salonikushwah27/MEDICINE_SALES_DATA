Medicine Sales Dashboard | Power BI
An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing global pharmaceutical sales performance across regions, manufacturers, drugs, and dosage forms for the period 2023–2024.
📊 Key Metrics
Total Revenue: 26.08M
Total Units Sold: 1M
Manufacturers Tracked: 1K (Company V, W, X, Y, Z)
Dosage Forms: 1K (Cream, Tablet, Injection, Capsule, Syrup)
🔍 Dashboard Insights
Revenue by Region: South America leads with ~6M in revenue, followed by North America, Europe, Asia, and Africa
Revenue Trend (2023–2024): Consistent upward trend, revenue grew from ~12M to 14M+ YoY
Top Manufacturers by Revenue: Company V ranked highest, followed by Company Y, X, W, and Z
Top 5 Drugs by Revenue: Drug B, Drug C, Drug A, Drug D, and Drug E emerged as top performers
Units Sold by Dosage Form: Cream (231K) leads, followed by Tablet (221K), Injection (211K), Capsule (193K), and Syrup
🧰 Features
Interactive slicers for Customer Type (Clinics, Hospitals, Online Pharmacy, Pharmacy, Supermarkets) and Manufacturer
Year filter (2023/2024) for trend comparison
KPI cards for quick summary of revenue, units sold, and manufacturer count
Donut chart for dosage form distribution
Horizontal bar chart for manufacturer-wise revenue ranking

Power BI · Power Query · DAX
